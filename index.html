The Day of Exordium



IEEE SB GECBH celebrated its 15th Anniversary on September 27, 2020, with an official meeting attended by both IEEE and non-IEEE members. The event began with the recital of the IEEE Code of Ethics, followed by welcome addresses from Dr. Birenjith P.S. and Dr. N. Vijayakumar, who praised the branch's contributions to the technical development of the college. Mrs. Sarada Jayakrishnan (Chair, IEEE Kerala Section) congratulated the branch for successfully organizing 10 pre-events and a three-day anniversary conclave. Dr. K C Raveendranathan, the founding Branch Counselor, was honored as the Guest of Honor. The competition results were announced, with the Department of Electronics & Communications emerging as the overall winners, followed by the Department of Mechanical Engineering as runners-up.

The event also featured a special video with messages from past Chairpersons, evoking nostalgia among alumni and faculty. Nandan S (Chair, Student Activities Committee, IEEE Kerala Section) gave an energetic felicitation speech, and all former Branch Counselors were recognized for their contributions. The meeting concluded with a vote of thanks by Adhithyaa G, Chair of IEEE SB GECBH, who acknowledged the collective efforts of the ExCom members in making the event a grand success.
